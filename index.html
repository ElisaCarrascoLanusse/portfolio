Featured Projects

ProCase Displays: Ecommerce and Marketing

ProCase Displays specializes in creating customizable acrylic display cases for all sorts of memorabilia. Merging style with functionality, they aim to elevate collections to the next level. My role in this project has mainly been the creation of the ecommerce website as well as the marketing strategies and social media management.

Labor Violations: Data Analysis and Visualization

Part of the International Institute of St. Louis, the Anti-Trafficking In Persons team works to provide resources and support to victims of human trafficking. This project was focused on labor-trafficking to analyze and visualize official data on labor violations in Eastern Missouri. The goal was to identify patterns and trends where domestic and international workers might be at particular risk of trafficking.

Laboratoria: Unlocking Engagement

Since it’s creation in 2014, Laboratoria has been consistently growing and adapting in their mission to provide tech education for low income women in Latin America. As part of this growth, a focus on community engagement has become vital. I helped revamp the Partnerships newsletter and improve the design of communications to foster engagement.
